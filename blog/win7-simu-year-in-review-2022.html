      # Win7 Simu year in review - 2022

      1/1/2023, 12:40:40 PM

      Cover image by Tron Le via Unsplash (opens new window)

      Another year passed by, how was it for you? Hope you had a wonderful year. For Win7 Simu, 2022 was amazing. For a hobby project that was started out just for fun and learning, it definitely has been growing surprisingly thanks to my effort the love and support from you all. So in this post, let us have a look back at some amazing numbers that Win7 Simu achieved in 2022.

      # The amazing numbers

      • 4.3M users downloaded the app from Google Play (opens new window)
      • 30K users downloaded the app from Huawei AppGallery (opens new window)
      • 29K users gave an average rating of 4.1/5
      • 15K users left a review
      • 500K accounts created in the app
      • 13 version updates were released
      • 50 contributors helped out with the translations on Crowdin (opens new window)
